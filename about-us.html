Bible Conversations: Teesside is a community-focused initiative that’s all about making the Bible accessible and relatable for anyone who wants to find out more about it – and make up their own mind on it.

Who’s behind Bible Conversations: Teesside?

Bible Conversations: Teesside is a joint initiative between UK-based charity Bible Society and churches in Teesside, brought together through the Transforming-Teesside-Together movement.

Bible Society was founded in 1804. Since then, it’s worked to translate, distribute and share the Bible all around the world.

Today, we’re inviting people to explore the Bible in fresh, honest and relevant ways – creating space for you to discover how this source of ancient wisdom can speak into your spiritual search.

But what’s the thinking behind Bible Conversations: Teesside?

Why does Bible Conversations: Teesside exist? And why now?

More and more people are interested in finding out more about spirituallity, belief and and the Bible.

But reading the Bible isn’t without its challenges. The language is tricky to get your head around. And some of it feels really outdated, right?

And yet, it’s also stood the test of time. And billions of people around the world continue to read it, learn from it and get inspired by it. And maybe it has something to offer you today – a message of hope, a word of encouragement or a story that helps you understand why you’re here.

Bible Conversations: Teesside creates a space for those who are interested in finding out more about the Bible to try it out for themselves.

Meet people, chat about the Bible and figure out how you feel about it.

Bible Conversations: Teesside likes to keep things simple

It’s here to help people with a genuine desire to try the Bible to step back from daily distractions and find space to read a little bit of it – without having to do tons of homework or get caught up in the context.

Instead, it’s a place to have honest conversations about the Bible – a space to explore the Bible together in Teesside.

Meet, build community, discover the Bible.
