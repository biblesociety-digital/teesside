What is The Bible Conversation?

It's a chance to meet people, chat about the Bible and figure out how you feel about it by listening to four short Bible stories across four free sessions.

They’re hosted by local churches across Teesside – by people who don’t have all the answers but ask the right questions to help you dig into the Bible yourself.

Join The Bible Conversation to:

  • check_boxBuild community with others looking to explore the Bible
  • check_boxGain an understanding of 4 Bible stories
  • check_boxChat about what the Bible says about people, God and life

What happens during The Bible Conversation?

No two conversations are the same. But they all follow the same simple structure:

  • check_boxYou meet up as a small group hosted by a local church
  • check_boxYou listen to 1 short Bible story per session
  • check_boxYou chat about what it says about God, people and life

Each group has two hosts to keep the conversation on track – with space for 5–15 participants.

What will I cover in each session?

During each event, you’ll listen to a short story from either Luke or Acts – two books that talk about Jesus. Then, your host asks your group some questions such as:

  • question_markWhat do you like or dislike about the story?
  • question_markWhat does this story teach us about God?
  • question_markWhat does it reveal about people or about yourself?

There are no right or wrong answers. And there’s no pressure to speak either, if you’d just prefer to listen.

This isn’t a formal teaching session. It’s all about finding a space where you can explore the Bible for yourself – to see how you feel about it and discover what it says to you today.

Got questions?

Do I need to book?

You’re absolutely welcome to turn up to a session near you without booking. But we recommend securing your slot via Eventbrite, so your hosts know what to expect in terms of numbers. Plus, they’ll be able to make sure you have all the information you need ahead of meeting.

Can I bring a friend along?

Definitely – the more the merrier.

Why not share this link and invite them to sign up too?

How long do Bible Conversations last?

Each session is designed to last around 45 minutes. They’ll be wrapped up by the hour, unless you want to chat for longer.

How do the sessions fit together?

There’s absolutely zero pressure to contribute.

The main thing is that you feel comfortable and relaxed. And don’t worry: you won’t be put on the spot with any tricky Bible questions. And your hosts won’t ask you to read the Bible aloud if you don’t want to.

However, this is designed as an open discussion, so we hope everyone feels confident to share their thoughts on the stories if they wish to.

How do I sign up?

Registration is quick and easy.

Locate The Bible Conversation group that works for you via our postcode search tool and register via Eventbrite.

Keep an eye on your inbox for any information you need once you’ve signed up.
