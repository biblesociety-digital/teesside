What is The Bible Course?

The Bible’s an ancient book spanning thousands of years. And it’s central to two major world religions. But what actually happens in the Bible?

The Bible Course shows you how a collection of ancient writings fit together to tell one big story about God, the world and our place in it. Across eight course films with Bible experts and space for discussion, you’ll start to make sense of the Bible, uncover its core message and see how it’s changing lives today.

Join The Bible Course to:

  • check_boxTake a whistlestop tour through the Bible’s big story
  • check_boxLearn together and discuss your thoughts through 8 interactive sessions
  • check_boxTake your understanding of the Bible to the next level

What will I cover in each session?

The course is split into 8 sessions which outline the major action points of the Bible.

  • Session 1:an introduction to the Bible
  • Session 2:the story begins – a look at the first book of the Bible
  • Session 3:explore a story of freedom for the early Israelite people
  • Session 4:exploring faith and the monarchy in early Israel
  • Session 5:a story of loss, political turmoil and exile
  • Session 6:Jesus enters the scene
  • Session 7:see how Jesus’ life inspired Christianity
  • Session 8:the closing chapter – explore the last book of the Bible

Each session includes video teaching, opportunity to discuss a Bible passage as a small group and a closing reflection.

Each group has two hosts to keep the conversation on track – with space for 5–15 participants.

Got questions?

Do I need to book?

We advise booking onto The Bible Course at a time and place that suits you, so your hosts can get in touch with you to share any information you need to know.

And you only need to register via Eventbrite once: you’ll automatically be signed up for all eight sessions.

However, you’re still very welcome to pop along to a session near you even if you haven’t had a chance to register.

Can I bring a friend along?

Absolutely – why not share this page with them, so they can sign up and join in?

How long does each session last?

Each video is roughly 30 to 40 minutes long – with a break halfway through to discuss a short Bible verse that’s related to the session.

Most sessions will be around an hour long, but there’s flexibility if the conversation’s flowing.

How do I sign up?

Locate your closest group using via our postcode search tool. Registration only takes a few moments via Eventbrite.
